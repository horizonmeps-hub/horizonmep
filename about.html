About Horizon MEP

Horizon MEP is a U.S.-focused MEP engineering firm delivering coordinated, permit-ready Mechanical, Electrical, Plumbing, Fire Protection and Fire Alarm designs for architects, developers, and contractors.

Who We Are

We partner with project teams across the United States to deliver practical, construction-ready MEP solutions aligned with real-world build conditions and jurisdictional requirements.

Our focus is simple: reduce friction during permitting and construction through clear documentation, disciplined coordination, and code-first engineering.

How We Work

  • Early review of architectural intent and jurisdictional codes
  • Integrated MEP coordination to minimize conflicts
  • Permit-ready documentation aligned with AHJ expectations
  • Responsive support through reviews and revisions

Credentials & Capability

Our team includes licensed engineers, Title-24 specialists, and designers experienced in NFPA, NEC, IMC, IPC, and local amendments.

  • Multi-state permitting experience
  • Residential, commercial, and mixed-use projects
  • Fire protection and fire alarm coordination
  • Energy compliance and documentation support
